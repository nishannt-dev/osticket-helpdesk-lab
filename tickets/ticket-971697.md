## Internal Notes (Agent: James Carter)

Received urgent ticket from David Patel reporting laptop not charging with battery critically low. Prioritised due to upcoming meeting. Steps taken:

1. Visited David's desk immediately given the urgency and 4% battery level
2. Visually inspected the power adapter and charging cable -- no visible physical damage on the cable
3. Checked the charging port on the laptop -- no debris or visible damage found
4. Tested the laptop with a spare power adapter from IT inventory -- laptop began charging immediately
5. Confirmed the original adapter was faulty by testing it on another identical laptop -- no charge
6. Ran Windows battery health diagnostic -- battery itself was healthy, 94% of original capacity remaining
7. Issued David the replacement adapter from IT inventory as a permanent swap
8. Logged the faulty adapter as defective in the IT asset records and flagged for disposal
9. Confirmed charging indicator was active and battery was climbing before leaving the desk

Root cause: The original power adapter failed. The laptop hardware and battery were confirmed healthy. Fault was isolated to the adapter unit.
